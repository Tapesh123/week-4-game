Crystal Collector


Here's how to play this game!


  1. You will be given a random # at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals you see below. By clicking on the Crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  3. You in the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score gets above the random number.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  5. Each time when the game starts the game will change the values of each crystal
